Basic Upper/Lower Bodybuilding Split

  1. Day 1: Upper - Chest Focused

    • Chest Fly
    • Inclined Chest Press
    • Lateral Raise
    • Bicep Curl
    • Barbell Row
    • Lat Prayer

  2. Day 2: Lower - Quad Focused

    • Leg Extensions
    • Hack Squats
    • Hip Abduction
    • Weighted Fold Over
  3. Rest

  4. Day 3: Upper - Back Focused

    • Pull-Ups
    • Cable Row
    • Chest Press
    • Skull crushers
    • Upright Row
    • Bicep Curls

  5. Day 4: Lower - Hamstring Focused

    • Stiff-Legged Dead-lifts
    • Hip Thrust
    • Leg Curls
    • Leg Press
  6. Rest
